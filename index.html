About Me

One of the most popular dog breeds in America, the Golden Retriever is a gentle, loyal, and stable family dog. They enjoy pleasing their masters, so obedience training can be fun. The breed excels in obedience competition, but can be overexuberant and distractible.

Goldens are outstanding family pets because they are patient and kind with very small children, and generally friendly with everyone. However, they need people a lot, and if isolated from human contact or left alone for long periods of time, they can become mischievous.
